Favorite Aesthetics

  1. Frutiger Aero

    I chose this aesthetic as I enjoy this nostalgic style, with the bubbly, optimistic visual design alongside a color scheme mostly focused on cool colors.

  2. Vaporwave

    The vibes and tones of vaporwave are awfully nostalgic and calming in a way. Despite the strange style to it, it also feels quite calming to it.

  3. Frutiger Aurora

    Alongside with Frutiger Aero, Frutiger Aurora has a beautiful design to it. With the inspiration with the Aurora Borealis, this aesthetic utilizes beautiful gradients using mostly warm colors, with a different colored background, usually with cooler colors.

  4. Liminal Spaces

    Despite being a scaredycat in real life, I do enjoy a bit of horror. This includes this aesthetic, where it shows the uncanniness of places, instead of living beings instead.

  5. Analog Horror

    Another neat horror-themed aesthetic, analog horror has been a recent classic when it comes to horror styled content. There has been many creators that use the style to design a story with such rivoting and terrifying premise.


Favorite Drinks:

  • Dr. Pepper

    Introducted to it by my mother, this drink has a sense of twist in taste. Many have described the taste being like that of medicine, but I don't see it as that. Instead, it has a small distinct flavor cherry in it.

  • Sparkling Ice, or Sparkling Water

    Despite many people thinking Sparkling Water is expensive, my family loves these cheaper sparkling water, named "Sparkling Ice". This drink is basically if you flavor water for a bit, when later carbonate it into a soft drink-like state, you get Sparkling Ice.

  • Tea

    It always been a long standing argument on whether people enjoy tea. For me, I do enjoy tea, both in their soothing tone along with the benefit of cleansing my body.

  • Juices From Fruits, like apple or orange juice

    Simple really. I enjoy Fruit Juices as they can vary in flavor, depending on the manufacturing company or the types of fruits and/or ingredients they use in the drinks.

  • Water

    A simple drink, a simple decision. Water is alwats refreshing to me, especially when cold. If you feel you want to drink something simple, to hydrate and refresh yourself for something, some water can help.
